Hyper Store Sales Dashboard (Power BI)
Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI sales dashboard designed to analyze the performance of a hyper store across multiple dimensions, including revenue, profit, products, customer segments, and categories. The dashboard enables business stakeholders to make data-driven decisions through clear KPIs and visual insights.

Business Objectives

Track overall sales and profitability

Identify top and underperforming products

Analyze monthly revenue and sales trends

Compare performance across regions and years

Understand revenue distribution by segment and category

Key KPIs

Total Revenue

Net Profit

Units Sold

Total Cost

Dashboard Features

Monthly trend analysis for revenue, profit, and units sold

Top 5 best-selling and worst-selling products

Revenue breakdown by:

Customer Segment

Product Category

Interactive slicers for:

Year

Region (North America, South America)

Tools & Technologies

Power BI Desktop

Data modeling & DAX measures

Interactive visualizations

Key Insights

A small number of products contribute a significant portion of total revenue

Consumer segment generates the highest share of revenue

Certain products consistently underperform and may require pricing or inventory review

Seasonal trends are visible in monthly revenue and unit sales
